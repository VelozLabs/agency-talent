# Session C-01 — Brand Identity
**Agent**: Brand Guardian (`design-brand-guardian`)
**Input**: C-00 brief
**Phase**: 2 — Concepting

---

## Agent Directive

Read the C-00 brief. Your job is to translate the product's core metaphor into a visual identity system. You are NOT designing screens yet — that's C-03. You are establishing the foundation everything else builds on: color, type, design anti-rules, and the visual language of the metaphor.

Do not begin with a logo. Begin with a question: *what does [PRODUCT METAPHOR] look like as a material?*

---

## Deliverables

### 1. Metaphor Translation

- What is the product metaphor? (from C-00)
- What are its material properties? (texture, temperature, weight, light behavior)
- How do those properties translate to screen? (color temperature, typography weight, corner radii, motion character)
