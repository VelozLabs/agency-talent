## Gate Criteria

Gates pause the team for human review before proceeding. A gate is not cleared until Harold explicitly signs off.

### Gate 02 — Reddit Strategy Approval
- [ ] Subreddit targeting confirmed (primary tier: r/f150, r/FordTrucks; secondary: r/Trucks, r/MechanicAdvice)
- [ ] 90/10 content ratio plan accepted (no promotional posts for 90 days)
- [ ] Karma-building timeline is realistic given founder's bandwidth
- [ ] AMA concept validated: Harold as founder/OBD-II expert is the right voice
- [ ] Confirm no Reddit TOS exposure from diagnostic content

### Gate 03 — TikTok Strategy Approval
- [ ] Content pillars (40/30/20/10 mix) confirmed for truck audience
- [ ] Hook library reviewed — no misleading diagnostic claims
- [ ] Creator partnership budget confirmed (or deferred to paid phase)
- [ ] FYP optimization and cross-posting to Reels/Shorts validated
- [ ] Disclaimers integrated into content plan

### Gate 05 — Paid Amplification Budget Sign-Off
- [ ] Monthly paid ceiling approved by Harold
- [ ] Reddit Promoted Posts vs. TikTok Ads allocation agreed
- [ ] Minimum 3 creative variants per campaign before scaling
- [ ] Attribution model agreed (UTM structure, pixel placement)
- [ ] Legal review: no misleading diagnostic claims in ad copy

### Gate 07 — Final Orchestrator Verdict
- [ ] Reality Checker score ≥ 7/10
- [ ] Ford C&D risk rated acceptable
- [ ] OBD-II lockout timeline understood and MVP launch window confirmed
- [ ] LLM accuracy / disclaimer framework validated
- [ ] 90-day launch roadmap signed off

## Key Constraints for All Agents

1. **Read-only architecture** — Never suggest writing to ECUs, modules, or vehicle configs. TruckPulse is read-only by design.
2. **No FORScan references** — Do not recommend, reference, or build on FORScan, FDRS, IDS, or Ford proprietary protocols.
3. **Legal firewall is a feature** — Lead with "legally clean, warranty-safe" as a competitive advantage in all messaging.
4. **Disclaimer on all diagnostic content** — Every consumer-facing diagnostic claim must include professional verification language.
5. **Target persona: Lariat+ owner** — All community and content strategy should speak to premium truck owners, not general mechanics.
